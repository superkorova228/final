For those infected with the Cogito virus

"Ergo Proxy" is a complex, controversial, and ambiguous series. You could call it "neo-cyberpunk," or "a psychological road movie," or "a futuristic parable with a philosophical twist and gunplay"... It's all mixed together, sometimes too much. Besides the plot and characters, "Ergo Proxy" contains a fair number of allusions, quotes, and references to cultural, philosophical, and other phenomena. Each viewer decides for themselves: simply watch without paying attention to the subtext, limit themselves to their existing knowledge, or dive into an abyss of unfamiliar information. This website aims to fill in the blanks and provide a basis for reflection for those who cannot help but ponder.
